Sauteed Veal Chops

Description

This is a very simple and good recipe for veal with a Mediterranean flair.

Ingredients

  • 4 center loin veal chops (approximately 10 oz, each)
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp chopped parsley
  • 1 tbsp chopped onion
  • 4 bay leaves
  • 4 whole garlic cloves, peeled
  • 4 sprigs of fresh thyme (can substitute 1 tsp dried)
  • 2 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 3/4 cup chicken stock

Steps

  1. Season chops with salt and pepper
  2. Dredge chops with flour on flat surface
  3. Brown chops in olive oil over medium-high heat for roughly five minutes per side
  4. Add onions, garlic, and herbs and stir-fry for three minutes
  5. Pour balsamic around chops and reduce by half
  6. Add stock and simmer for 15 to 20 minutes or until tender
  7. Serve chops and pour remaining sauce onto them
