Jewish Pot Roast

Description

A Jewish pot roast from mom's coworker

Ingredients

  • 4 lbs chuck roast (preferably 2" thick and marbled)
  • 2 large onions
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp ground cloves
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 2 lemons

Steps

  1. Set oven to 300 F
  2. Fry and then remove onions
  3. Brown the meat
  4. Juice the lemons, mix in remaining ingredients together, then cover the meat
  5. Add back the onions
  6. Roast for 3 hours, covered, checking occasionally to ensure it doesn't dry out
