Pork Butt Bone-In

Description

A simple and tasty pork butt recipe

Ingredients

  • 4 1/2 - 5 lbs pork butt, bone-in (must be marbled--shoulder an acceptable substitute)
  • 1 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 2 1/2 cups water
  • onions
  • potatoes
  • turkey gravy mix (chicken is too mild, pork and beef don't seem to pair well)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 300 F
  2. Fry sides of roast, then remove
  3. Fry onions in fat drippings
  4. Return roast, cover, cook for 4 hours
  5. Slice potatoes 1/4" thin, peeled
  6. cook them in broth on the stove

Notes

Pairs well with stir-fried red cabbage
